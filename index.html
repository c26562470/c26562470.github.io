Congratulations on making it this far.

But, the job isn't finished.



Mysteries unravel in the pale moon's gleam,

underneath the stars, where whispers teem.

radiant dreams unfold in the midnight's core,

infinite possibilities the night has in store.

Nebulous shadows dance, a mystical scene,

echoes of the universe, where realms convene.



Infinite stars adorn the cosmic sky,

luminous constellations catch the eye.

Ethereal beauty in the night's vast sea,

xanadu unveiled in celestial glee.



Whispers of the wind through the trees,

overhead, the moonlight gently frees.

Outstretched branches in a quiet mood,

dancing in a tranquil, wooded solitude.



Dated 1951.
